Company Information

Boilerplate

Tide Forge Games is an independent game development studio dedicated to crafting immersive worlds and shaping captivating stories. Founded in 2025, our mission is to blend boundless imagination with meticulous craftsmanship, creating robust, beautiful, and deeply engaging interactive experiences that challenge the mind and capture the heart. We believe in the power of community-driven development and strive to build games that foster connection and creativity.

Based in Fort Atkinson, WI, USA, Tide Forge Games is committed to pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment, one forged world at a time.

Game Press Kits

Virtual Playmat

Bridge the gap between physical and digital play.

Virtual Playmat is a revolutionary platform designed to seamlessly integrate physical and virtual components for any board game, card game, or RPG. It allows players to connect globally, using webcams for physical cards or fully virtual assets within the same session, fostering a truly hybrid tabletop experience.

Fact Sheet

Developer: Tide Forge Games
Publisher: Tide Forge Games
Platforms: PC (Steam), Mac
Release Date: Q4 2025 (Planned)
Price: TBD
Website: tideforgegames.com/virtual-playmat

Key Features

  • Unique Hybrid Play: Combine physical and virtual assets seamlessly.
  • Direct P2P Connection: High-quality video & audio for real-time interaction.
  • Community Driven: Full Steam Workshop integration for custom content.
  • Intuitive Interface: Easy to learn, powerful tools for game masters and players.
